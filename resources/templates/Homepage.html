Welcome to "NiGiLe"

This is a website for the professors to login and register. The project was made for professors to keep in track with their classes, students and grades.

After the login or registration the professor will be redirected to his profile and from then on he will be able to manage all sorts of things.


Are you an admin with an account already? Then proceed in the login option.
Are you an admin with no account? Register in the Admin Registration option with the special key given to you by the administration office.
